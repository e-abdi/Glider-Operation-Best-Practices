# Ballasting

Ballasting adjusts a glider's mass and mass distribution so that it is
neutrally buoyant and correctly trimmed in the water it will actually be
deployed into. It is a tank procedure, not a field one: get it wrong and the
glider wastes buoyancy-drive energy fighting an unwanted heaviness or
lightness for the rest of the deployment, or in a bad case can't dive or
climb at all. Ballasting follows lab test and precedes deployment.

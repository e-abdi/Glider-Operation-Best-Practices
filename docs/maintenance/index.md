# Maintenance

Maintenance keeps a glider ready for its next mission: what to check before
every deployment, what to service after every recovery, and what only needs
attention on its own schedule (pump service life, O-ring dry-out, sacrificial
anodes). Most of the detailed how-to already lives on each component's page
under [Glider Components](../glider-components/index.md) — the schedules
here pull those facts together by **when**, not by **what**, and link back
to the component pages for the full procedure.

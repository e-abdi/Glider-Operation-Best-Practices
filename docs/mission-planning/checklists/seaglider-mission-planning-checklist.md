## Mission definition

- [ ] Project description and mission objectives written down
- [ ] Special operational considerations noted
- [ ] Timeline agreed: earliest deployment, latest recovery
- [ ] Operational risk assessment completed and summarized
- [ ] Major show-stoppers assessed, with a plan B for each
- [ ] Permits identified and obtained *(if any)*

## Environment

- [ ] Expected currents in the operating area characterized &emsp;<small>*can the glider stem them? (~40 cm/s depth-averaged is the practical limit)*</small>
- [ ] Depth range of the area mapped; bathymetry maps prepared if needed
- [ ] Density/stratification range checked against the available VBD budget
- [ ] Ice climatology / ice maps reviewed *(high-latitude missions)*

## Roles & communications

- [ ] Client point of contact
- [ ] Operations manager / account rep
- [ ] Data QC engineer
- [ ] Project & field coordinator
- [ ] Deployment/recovery lead(s) with contact info
- [ ] Pilot(s) with contact info
- [ ] Communication channels agreed for deployment, piloting, and recovery

## Sensors & sampling

- [ ] Installed sensors listed with serial numbers and calibration dates
- [ ] Acquisition needs per sensor defined &emsp;<small>*depth ranges, sampling frequency — becomes the `science` / `scicon.sch` file*</small>

## Endurance & logistics

- [ ] Endurance calculation done for the planned sampling and dive depth
- [ ] Deployment and recovery vessel(s) identified
- [ ] Emergency recovery option identified

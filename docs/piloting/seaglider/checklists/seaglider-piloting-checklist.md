## Each piloting session

- [ ] Check glider status: latest `comm.log` entries and the current `cmdfile` state &emsp;<small>*did the last call complete? what directive is staged?*</small>
- [ ] Review glider health: battery consumption and minimum voltage, internal pressure/vacuum, humidity, Iridium call quality
- [ ] Review the dive plots (vis or piloting tools): dive symmetry, vertical velocity, pitch/roll behavior
- [ ] Check the altimeter is finding the bottom and is sensibly tuned *(if in use)*
- [ ] Trim if needed; **log every `cmdfile` change** on the agreed team channel
- [ ] Confirm science sensors are producing data of expected quality; watch for anomalies
- [ ] Plot the latest surfacing positions; confirm the glider is tracking its waypoints
- [ ] Check ship-traffic and current forecasts along the track for upcoming risks
- [ ] Update the endurance estimate against plan &emsp;<small>*amp-hours consumed per day vs. remaining mission*</small>
- [ ] Confirm mission objectives are being met; propose plan adjustments if not
- [ ] Fill in the dive/pilot log

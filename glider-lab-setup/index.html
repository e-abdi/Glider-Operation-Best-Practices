    Glider Lab Setup¶

    The glider lab is the primary workspace for all pre-deployment preparation activities. Every mission begins here, and the condition of this space directly affects the reliability of hardware going into the field. Maintaining a clean, organised, and properly equipped lab reduces the risk of human error, component damage, and missed pre-flight steps. All personnel working in the lab must follow established ESD protocols, chemical-handling procedures, and equipment accountability practices before any glider work begins.
